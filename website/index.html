About

We are a workers cooperative, set up to provide IT services to people, based around their needs.

  • Cooperative by name...

    Our approach is to work with people to help them with their IT needs, finding solutions that fit them, their organisation and their budgets.

  • Experienced

    We have experience of looking after large multinational banking systems, small voluntary and community organisations, and everything in between.

  • Open

    We are advocates of open source software and open and transparent ways of working, which protects our customers, giving them freedom to choose their IT suppliers, and avoiding getting tied into a single vendor.

  • Principled

    As a Cooperative we're guided by the seven cooperative principles in the way we run our business and live in our communities.

Langdale/Ambleside Mountain Rescue Team

Long term Drupal website support

When Google Maps first began letting you drop markers on a map, we got in touch with the Langdale/Ambleside Mountain Rescue Team to suggest we could map their incidents. This turned into a project where 15 developers went to the lakes to spend a weekend creating the new website as volunteers. Many years later we still look after site and recently managed an upgrade from Drupal 8 to 9.

  • Client: Langdale/Ambleside Mountain Rescue Team
  • Category: Drupal, Open Source Software, Website

Bradford Environmental Education Services Website

Long term Drupal website support

Recently upgraded to Drupal 9, we've been looking after the BEES website for a number of years. The site itself is fairly complex with a vast photo gallery, and a need to publish regular activity programmes and updates.

  • Client: Bradford Environmental Education Service
  • Category: Drupal, Open Source Software, Website
